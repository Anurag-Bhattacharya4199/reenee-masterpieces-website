About Me:

Hello, I’m Rini from India, and I’m thrilled to welcome you to my website. Raised in a middle-class, educated family, I have always cherished our rich heritage and culture. My passion for Indian handicrafts drives me to infuse traditional artistry with my own creative touches. My journey is deeply inspired by my son, whose love and patience fuel my dedication to this craft. His encouragement has empowered me to pursue my passion for handicrafts at this stage in my life. I invite you to explore and enjoy my collection of handcrafted masterpieces, each created with meticulous care and a personal touch.
